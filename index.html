King Gnu CEN+RAL Tour 2026

King Gnu has announced the biggest tour in the band’s history, titled the King Gnu CEN+RAL Tour 2026, which is a cross-country tour in Asia featuring 29 performances over a period of five months (February – June 2026).

This tour is the largest in the history of King Gnu in terms of the number of performances, kicking off in Japan in February and concluding in Seoul in June 2026.

ABOUT KING GNU

King Gnu is a Japanese rock band formed in 2013, known for blending rock, jazz, funk, and electronic music into a unique, genre-crossing sound. The band consists of Satoru Iguchi (vocals & keyboard), Daiki Tsuneta (vocals & guitar), Kazuki Arai (bass), and Yu Seki (drums).

They gained mainstream popularity with hits like “Hakujitsu” and “Teenager Forever”, and are celebrated for their dynamic live performances and artistic music videos. King Gnu has quickly become one of Japan’s most influential contemporary music acts.
